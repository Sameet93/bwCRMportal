Frequently Asked Questions

  1. I can see the wireless network, but I can't log in – what's wrong?

    Try the following:

    1. Turning your WiFi off and on again
    2. Forget the wireless network from your device Wi-Fi settings (delete it) and then add it again.
    3. Rebooting your device
    It’s possible that your location may be causing interference with the Wi-Fi signal – try moving to another location to attempt to connect to the service.

  2. I can't see the wireless network – what can I do?

    • Try moving to another location and scanning again, if you are in a basement or surrounded with high buildings was that could also cause poor signal.
    • Make sure that wireless is turned on in your device settings, it’s possible that it has been turned-off accidentally.

  3. Network connection keeps dropping, how can i fix this?

    • You may be moving out of range of the WiFi. Check your device’s WiFi signal strength indicator and move to a different location.
    • Some devices may automatically disconnect you from the wireless network when they have not been used for a while.
    • Try another location.It’s also possible that your location may be causing interference with the WiFi signal.

  4. How long can I use the BrightWave WiFi for?

    You have 50MB of complentary free data per day per device. So as long as you havnt used-up the daily amount you can browse the web for as long as you wish! Once your data has been depleted you may top-up to continue browsing.

  5. I can see the wireless network, but I can't log in – what's wrong?

    Try the following:

    1. Turning your WiFi off and on again
    2. Forget the wireless network from your device Wi-Fi settings (delete it) and then add it again.
    3. Rebooting your device

  6. What is an OTP and how do I use it?

    • OTP stands for One Time Pin. It is a method of verification to make sure that you are really you – and not a robot.
    • When you receive the SMS, click on the link and it will send you to a web page.
      Go back to your WiFi settings, click on 'BrightWave WiFi' and log in. If you are using an Apple Device. You may receive the SMS split into two parts: a message; and a preview message. Once you have clicked on the preview message; there is no need to click on the message again because you wil already be verified. After clicking on 'tap to preview'; go back to your wifi settings and log in. Alternatively, you can open your browser and you will be redirected to the login page.

  7. Is the Wi-Fi unlimited?

    The free Wi-Fi is limited to 50MB per day. However you can top-up more data on the 'Top Up' page to increase your data. Please note: each data package lasts for a 30 days only afterwhich it is deleted.

  8. Forgot my Password, how can I login?

    On the Registration/Login page follow these steps:

    1. Click on 'Forgot Password'
    2. Enter the phone number you used to register - make sure it starts with '27'
    3. You will receive an SMS with a link. Click on that link and you will be redirected to change your password

  9. How can I start browsing the web after I have registered?

    After logging in successfully, you will land on the dashboard. Click on 'Start Surfing' to begin browsing the web.

  10. How much data do I have left?/ How much data have I used? (Data balance)

    On the home page of the dsashboard there is a button with your balance displayed. You may also navigate to 'Top-Up' page. You will be able to view your usage as well as downnload a usage report.

  11. Can I register more than one account?

    No. However you can add multiple products to your account such as Home connect.

  12. My question isn't here.

    No problem! You can try the following options:

    1. Send us a message and we'll get back to you.
    2. Give us a call and our Support Team will assist you.
